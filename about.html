Shortly after, Lina worked for a state sponsored non-profit organization in Detroit, Michigan. Lina and her family moved from Windsor, Ontario to Greenville, South Carolina.


In the past 20+ years, Lina has been devoted to the business world. Her extensive entrepreneurial and managerial expertise comes from experience as an Executive Assistant CEO of a manufacturer company and VP of an award-winning accounting firm. She has also taken on leading roles in the banking industry and with nonprofit organizations. Her core values in all professional interactions and practices are integrity, openness, and loyalty.


Lina is a strong leader and has been active and supportive to her local community in Greenville, South Carolina. She is the president of Greenville Chinese Culture Association (GCCA). Under her leadership, the organization has grown from a 40 family member organization to triple its size, well over 120 families, within one year. Under her leadership, GCCA successfully hosted the Chinese New Year event with over 500 attendees including members and friends from surrounding areas. In addition to her leadership roles, Lina has taught Mandarin Chinese to both Chinese school students and to employees from large corporations including BMW and Michelin.
